Katara is a beautiful and intuitive musical instrument tuner for tuning your string instruments.

<b>Features:</b>
<ul>
<li>🎵 Real-time pitch detection with high accuracy</li>
<li>🎸 Support for multiple instruments (Guitar, Ukulele)</li>
<li>🔄 Visual tuning feedback with animated tuning pegs</li>
<li>🎯 Cents display for precise tuning</li>
<li>📊 Clear visual indicators for tuning direction</li>
<li>🔍 Manual mode for focusing on specific strings</li>
<li>🌙 Light/Dark theme support</li>
<li>🔒 Works completely offline - no internet permission required</li>
</ul>

<b>How to use:</b>
<ol>
<li>Launch and grant microphone permission</li>
<li>Select your instrument type</li>
<li>Play a string on your instrument</li>
<li>Follow the visual guidance:
  <ul>
  <li>🔴 Red/Orange: Too high - loosen the string</li>
  <li>🔵 Blue: Too low - tighten the string</li>
  <li>🟢 Green: String is in tune!</li>
  </ul>
</li>
<li>Toggle manual mode to focus on specific strings</li>
</ol>
